Types of Cybersecurity Attacks

What is a cyberattack? A cyberattack is an action designed to steal data, or change and destroy systems in a computer. As technology has advanced, cyberattacks have become more prevalent as businesses have digitized leading to more enticing information to be attacked.


The most common types of cyber attacks are:
  1. DoS (Denial of Service Attack): designed to overwhelm the resources of a system to the point where it is unable to reply to requests
  2. MITM (Man in the Middle): breaches in cybersecurity that make it possible for an attacker to eavesdrop on the data sent back and forth between two people, networks, or computers.
  3. Phishing Attacks: a malicious actor sends emails that seem to be coming from trusted, legitimate sources in an attempt to grab sensitive information from the target.
