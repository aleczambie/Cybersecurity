How to get in to Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is on the rise as we know, and it is a highly technical field. Hard skills are important and you will need them to land a role. Some skills you will need are networking fundamentals, operating systems, scripting and programming, and cloud computing.

Even though technical skills are required, you will need more. You will need to be able to collaborate and communicate with others clearly, especially when working with a team and explaining the technical aspects of the work to a non-computer wizard. Verbal communication skills, problem solving, and analytical skills are also needed.
