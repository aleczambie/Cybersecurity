What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity covers a big range of topics, but in general it is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are aimed at destroying, changing, and accessing the sensitive information and can sometimes lead to extortion, all of which will interrupt business operations. The problem with cybersecurity is that there are more devices than people, so attackers are becoming more and more innovative. A successful cyberattack is one that has layers and layers of security across the computers, networks, and data that you want to keep safe. The best way to achieve this is to have people, processes, and technology working together and complementing each other to create a strong defense against cyberattacks.
