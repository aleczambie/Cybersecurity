Ways to Protect Information

Cyberattacks can lead to loss of money, personal data, and even medical information. It also will harm your reputation and safety of an individual or company. Cybersecurity is preventing and responding to these threats that occur worldwide.


Here are a few tips to make sure you are safe:
  • Change passwords regularly, and do not share them!
  • Use antivirus and anti-malware solutions, and firewalls to block threats. And backup files often.
  • Do not click on links in texts or emails from people you do not know.
